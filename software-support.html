Software & Application Support

Assistance with university-licensed software, installations, and troubleshooting.

Your Guide to University Software & Applications

Accessing University Software

Both SIBT and WSUSCC students are required to use a range of essential software for their academic purposes. Information regarding specific software requirements for your course, along with installation instructions, will be provided by your teachers.

  • **Microsoft 365:** Access Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams, and more online or download desktop versions.

Login with your student/staff credentials to your respective university portal to find direct links and general installation guides.

For Western Sydney University Students: Access a variety of free software titles available to you through the official portal: Western Sydney Free Software Portal

Matlab Software Access: Follow this detailed guide for instructions on how to access and set up the Matlab software: Matlab Access Instructions (WSU IT)

Installation & Setup Assistance

SIBT and WSUSCC students can easily request software installation assistance from the iHelp team. We're here to guide you through installing and setting up university-provided software on your personal or university-issued devices, **based on the instructions provided by your teachers or official university IT guides.**

  • **Step-by-step guidance:** For common applications like Microsoft Office.
  • **Compatibility Checks:** Advising on system requirements.

Troubleshooting Software Issues

Experiencing problems with university software? Our team can help diagnose and resolve common issues.

  • **Application Crashes:** Why is your program closing unexpectedly?
  • **Feature Malfunctions:** A specific function isn't working as it should.
  • **Update Errors:** Issues occurring during or after software updates.

Before contacting us, try restarting the application or your device.
