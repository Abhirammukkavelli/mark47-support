Class Device & Software Requests (Staff)

Dedicated support for staff to request devices and software for teaching sessions.

Staff Device Booking and Software Request Form

All staff members are required to complete and submit this form along with the specific requirements for each class session. Please carefully fill in all fields to ensure smooth and timely processing of your request.

1. Submit Your Request Form

This form is used to submit requests for:

  • Booking of classroom devices
  • Software installation on classroom PCs, lab PCs, and laptops

Go to Request Form

2. Instructions for Completing the Form:

  • Provide detailed information about the class session, including date, time, and location.
  • Specify the device(s) you wish to book and the quantity required.
  • Clearly list any software that needs to be installed, including version numbers if applicable.
  • Double-check all entries before submission to avoid delays.

3. After You Submit:

After you submit the form, a team member will review your request and confirm the booking. You will also receive an estimated timeframe for software installation based on your requirements.

Thank you for your cooperation and timely submissions.
