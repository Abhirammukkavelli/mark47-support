Student Printer Usage Guide

Students enrolled on campus have access to student printers for both printing and scanning (including scanning to email or printing scanned documents). Each student receives $26 in print credit per session, which can be used throughout the session.

Printer Locations

  • Level 2, next to the iHelp Desk
  • Library, Level 8

Important Notes

  • Printing is only accessible via Campus Touchdown PCs.
  • Personal devices or laptops cannot connect directly to the student printers.
  • For any printing-related assistance, please visit the iHelp Desk on Level 2.

How to Print Documents

  1. Log in to a Campus Touchdown PC using your student credentials (Same username and password as your Wi-Fi access).
  2. Open the document you wish to print.
  3. Press Ctrl + P or select File > Print.
  4. Under Printer selection, choose "Student Printer".
  5. Select your print preferences (e.g., color or black & white).
  6. A PaperCut popup will display the document name and cost. Click OK to proceed.
  7. Go to the student printer and log in:

    Username: Your Student ID

    Password: Your date of birth in YYYYMMDD format

  8. On the printer screen, select Print Release.
  9. Your document will be listed. Select it and press Print to complete the job.

How to Scan Documents

  1. Log into the student printer using the same credentials (Student ID and YYYYMMDD).
  2. Select Access Device.
  3. Choose the appropriate scanning option based on your needs (e.g., scan to email).

Print Credit Information

  • Each student receives $26 print credit per session.
  • If your print credit is low or missing, please visit the iHelp Desk for a top-up.

For any questions or support, don't hesitate to reach out to the iHelp Desk on Level 2.
