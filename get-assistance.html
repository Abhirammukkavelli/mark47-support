Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by visiting the university's official password reset portal and following the on-screen instructions. A direct link is available on our 'Services' page under 'Account Management'.

My Wi-Fi isn't working, what should I do?

First, try restarting your device and ensuring you're connected to the correct university network (e.g., 'iHelp_Secure'). If issues persist, check our 'Announcements' for network outages or submit a support ticket.

Which software is available to students?

The university provides access to a range of software including Microsoft Office 365, Adobe Creative Cloud, and various specialized academic tools. Details and download links are available on the 'Services' page under 'Software & Licensing'.

How long does it take for a support ticket to be resolved?

Resolution times vary based on the complexity and urgency of the issue. Our average response time is within 4 business hours, and most common issues are resolved within 24-48 hours. You'll receive updates via email.
