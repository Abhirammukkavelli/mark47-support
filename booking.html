Schedule Your Technical Support Appointment

To ensure you receive dedicated and personalized technical assistance without waiting, we encourage you to schedule an appointment with our iHelp Tech Support team. Choose the method that best suits your needs.

How to Book an Appointment

Booking your dedicated support session with the iHelp Tech Support team is straightforward. Follow these steps to schedule your appointment for in-depth assistance via our online portal.

  • **Step 1: Choose Your Method:** Decide if you prefer a face-to-face or virtual (online) appointment.
  • **Step 2: Access Booking Portal:** Click the button below to go directly to our online booking system.
  • **Step 3: Select Service & Time:** Choose the type of support you need and pick a convenient date and time slot.
  • **Step 4: Confirm Details:** Provide your contact information and a brief description of your issue.

Virtual vs. Face-to-Face Appointments

We offer flexible appointment options to suit your needs:

  • **Virtual Appointments:** Convenient online sessions via Microsoft Teams. Ideal for software issues, troubleshooting, and general guidance from anywhere.
  • **Face-to-Face Appointments:** In-person sessions at the iHelp Desk. Recommended for hardware diagnostics, complex setup, or when a hands-on approach is required.

Before Your Appointment

To make the most of your scheduled session and ensure efficient resolution, please prepare the following:

  • **Issue Description:** Be ready to clearly explain your problem.
  • **Relevant Details:** Gather any error messages, screenshots, or steps you've already tried.
  • **Device & Credentials:** Have your device and university login details (if applicable) ready and accessible.

This preparation significantly helps our technicians assist you more quickly and efficiently.
