Ensuring effortless setup, secure access, and quick troubleshooting for all your university communications.

Student Email Account Setup & Password Reset Instructions

1. Why Your Student Email is Crucial

Important Communication Channel: Your student email is the primary and most crucial channel for all official communications with the university. Teachers and university staff will *only* communicate with you via your student email account.

Preferred Contact Method for Support: To ensure the quickest and most efficient assistance, it is a university-wide policy that students use their official university student email when contacting iHelp Tech Support. This practice is crucial for all parties involved – students, teachers, staff, and administrators – as it allows us to efficiently verify your identity and access necessary details to resolve your queries swiftly.

2. Student Email Accounts & Access

SIBT Students

Email Address: studentID@learning.sibt.nsw.edu.au

Password: Your default SIBT portal password

Access Webmail: Outlook Web Access

Important: First-time users must link their student email account through the SIBT Moodle portal.

To link your SIBT email account:

  • Log in to SIBT Moodle.
  • On the right-hand side panel, locate the Office 365 block and click on it.
  • Click on Microsoft 365 Connection Settings.
  • Under Connection Options, choose to link your Moodle account to your Microsoft 365 account.
  • Once successfully linked, you will see a confirmation message:
    “Your account has been linked to Office 365.”
WSUSCC Students

Email Address: studentID@student.westernsydney.edu.au

Password: Your Western Sydney University account password

Access Webmail: Outlook Web Access

3. Download Outlook App

Enhance your email experience by downloading the Microsoft Outlook application to your device.

  • Windows/macOS: Get Outlook for Desktop (Part of Microsoft 365 or standalone purchase)
  • iOS (iPhone/iPad): Download from App Store
  • Android: Download from Google Play

Note: SIBT and Western Sydney University students are eligible for free Microsoft 365 downloads when they log in to Office 365 with their student account. Please refer to your respective university's IT Services website for full details and download instructions.

4. Password Reset Instructions

SIBT Students

If you have forgotten your SIBT student email password:

  • Click “Forgot Password” on the login page.
  • Enter your Student ID to receive a password reset link to your nominated email or phone number.
  • Follow the instructions in the reset link to set a new password.

If you encounter any issues, please contact iHelp Tech Support for assistance.

WSUSCC Students

To reset your Western Sydney student email password:

  • Use the official Western account password reset link.
  • You must correctly answer your secret question during the process.
  • For password management: Western Account Password Management

Note: If you cannot recall your secret question, you must contact the Main Campus IT Support directly to have your password reset.

5. Main Campus IT Support (Western Sydney University)

  • Phone: (02) 9852 5111 or internal extension 5111
  • MyIT Portal: https://wsu.service-now.com/it
  • Email: itservicedesk@westernsydney.edu.au

For any additional technical support, students are encouraged to reach out to the respective IT support teams listed above.
