Network Login Information – SIBT & WSUSCC

This document provides the necessary Wi-Fi network login credentials for students, staff, and guests accessing Navitas networks at SIBT and WSUSCC campuses.

SIBT Students

Network Name: Navitas students

Username: Student ID

Password: Date of Birth in reverse (yyyymmdd)

SIBT Teachers

Network Name: Navitas Corporate

Username: firstname.lastname@navitas.com

Password: Navitas email password (Outlook password)

WSUSCC Students

Network Name: Navitas students

Username: wsuscc\studentid

Password: Date of Birth in reverse (yyyymmdd)

WSUSCC Staff / Teachers

Network Name: Navitas Corporate

Username: firstletter.lastname_wsuscc@navitas.com

Password: Navitas password (Outlook password)

Alternative Network (For WSUSCC Students, Staff, or Guests from Other Campuses)

Network Name: Eduroam

Username: Western account username

Password: Western account password

---

Important Notes:

  • Ensure that the date of birth is entered in the correct format for student logins: **yyyymmdd** (e.g., 20010421 for April 21, 2001).
  • Contact your IT support team if you experience issues connecting to any of the networks.
