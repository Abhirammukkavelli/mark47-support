About iHelp Tech Support

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to your success.

Our Story & Mission

At iHelp Tech Support, we are the dedicated technology backbone for the vibrant academic communities of **SIBT (Sydney Institute of Business and Technology)** and **WSUSCC (Western Sydney University Sydney City Campus)**. Our mission is deeply rooted in empowering every student and staff member within these institutions. We ensure seamless technological experiences, allowing you to focus on achieving your academic and professional goals without interruption. As an integral part of the university environment, we are committed to providing accessible, reliable, and friendly tech support tailored precisely to the unique needs of higher education.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled technical support, ensuring every student and staff member at SIBT and WSUSCC navigates their academic and professional journey with confidence. We are committed to providing seamless and efficient technology solutions, fostering an environment where innovation thrives without technical barriers.

Our Vision

To be recognized as an indispensable technology partner within the SIBT and WSUSCC communities, empowering users with intuitive solutions and proactive support. We envision a future where technology seamlessly facilitates learning, research, and administrative excellence, contributing directly to the success of our university and its constituents.

Our Values

Our core values are the guiding principles that shape every interaction and solution we provide. They reflect our unwavering commitment to the SIBT and WSUSCC communities and define who we are as a team dedicated to your success.

Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our interactions.

Excellence: Continuously striving for superior service and technical expertise.

Empathy: Understanding and addressing user needs with patience and care, ensuring a supportive experience throughout their university journey.

Innovation: Embracing new technologies and methods to enhance support.

Collaboration: Working together to find the best solutions.
