Service Charter & Mission

Professional technical governance for SIBT and WSUSCC academic operations.

Institutional Alignment

iHelp Tech Support is the primary technical infrastructure partner for the Sydney City Campus. We provide a bridge between complex university systems and the diverse needs of our international student body and academic staff. Our operations are strictly aligned with the IT Governance frameworks of Navitas and Western Sydney University.

Our Core Mandate

  • Maintaining Learning Continuity (Zoom/Teams/Wi-Fi).
  • Incident Resolution via ServiceNow & Ticket Tracking.
  • Compliance with University Data Privacy Policies.
  • Support for Hybrid & Online Learning Environments.

Operational Boundaries

To ensure focus on university systems, iHelp does not provide:

  • Hardware repairs for personal laptops/phones.
  • Support for non-university software.
  • Personal data recovery services.

Data & Privacy

We adhere to the Australian Privacy Principles. iHelp staff will never request your personal passwords and all remote assistance is performed via secure, university-approved channels.
